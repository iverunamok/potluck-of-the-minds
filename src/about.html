Why Potluck of the Minds?


The goal of Potluck of the Minds is to bring together those in our community with an urge to teach, learn, and share.


Values and Words We Hold Close.


  • Symposium: "A place to inspire thought and conversation about the abnormal and difficult topics that exist."

  • Enlighten: We strive for our attendees to leave feeling like they learned something new.

  • Community: There are a lot of people in this community with wisdom and knowledge to share. Let's learn from each other!
